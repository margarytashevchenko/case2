Race Information

The 2021 Diane's Run will be held Sunday, September 12, in Cheyenne starting at Prairie Park. All race fees are non-refundable and non-transferable, even in the event of inclement weather. Two races are offered: a 5K (3.1 miles) and a 10K (6.2 miles) event. We also offer a 1-mile Family Fun Walk.

Race Times

  • 5K Race starts promptly at 10 a.m.

  • 10K Race starts promptly at 11 a.m.

  • Family Fun Walk starts at noon

  • Celebration lunch and reception opens at 1:00 p.m.

Goodies and Stuff

  • A state-of-the-art running vest

  • A welcome pack with fund-raising advice and training tips

  • A goody bag with fun surprises

  • Refreshments from aid stations during and after the races

  • Tickets to a delicious post-race reception and meal

Notes

  • Registration will remain open until 10:00pm on Friday, September 10th.

  • Registration fees for the 5K and 10K races increase to $40 after Friday, September 3rd.

  • Race donations will be accepted through December 16th of this year.
